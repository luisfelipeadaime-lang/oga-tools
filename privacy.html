Privacy Policy

ClearSky Limited — Last updated: 2 April 2026

1. Introduction

ClearSky Limited ("ClearSky", "we", "us") operates the Oga Tools platform (tools.oga.earth) and related services, including integrations with the WhatsApp Business API. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

When you communicate with us via WhatsApp or use our platform, we may collect:

  • Contact information: phone number, name, and company affiliation
  • Message content: text messages sent to and received from our WhatsApp Business number
  • Usage data: timestamps, message delivery status, and interaction metadata
  • Business information: company name, industry, and professional role as provided by you or sourced from publicly available business directories

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide business communications
  • Manage our commercial relationships and CRM records
  • Improve our services and internal workflows
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. WhatsApp Business API

Our WhatsApp integration is powered by the Meta WhatsApp Business API. Messages you send to our WhatsApp Business number are processed by Meta Platforms, Inc. in accordance with their WhatsApp Privacy Policy. We store message content on our secure servers to facilitate ongoing business conversations.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Cloudflare) with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
