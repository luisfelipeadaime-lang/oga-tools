ARR RFP Overview

EY Afforestation, Reforestation & Revegetation — Feb 2026
After a successful spot RFP in 2025, EY is seeking proposals for near-term offtake of high-quality Nature Based removal projects. This RFP is focused on ARR projects, deliveries 2026-2030.

ACCEPTED METHODOLOGIES: VM0047, ISM Reforestation, ISM Agroforestry, Plan Vivo Acorn, M001 (Equitable Earth)
STANDARDS: VCS (Verra), Isometric, Plan Vivo (ex-post only), Equitable Earth
CARBON PROFILE: Removals only
VOLUME: 10,000 – 100,000 tCO₂e per year
STATUS: Projects under validation eligible (forward purchase)
RATING: Preferred minimum B (Sylvera/BeZero) or Tier 2 (Calyx)

EXCLUDED: REDD+, IFM, Gold Standard ARR, VM0033, VM0048, CAR Mexico, OFAC-sanctioned countries

DEADLINE: 27 February 2026, 23:59 GMT
SHORTLIST: March 2026 | DUE DILIGENCE: April-May 2026 | VERPA: June 2026

Biochar RFP Overview

EY Biochar Projects — Feb 2026
Same procurement cycle as ARR. Focused on biochar carbon removal projects.

ACCEPTED METHODOLOGIES: VM0044, Puro Biochar, ISM Biochar Production and Storage
STANDARDS: VCS (Verra), Puro Earth, Isometric
CARBON PROFILE: Removals only
VOLUME: 5,000 – 20,000 tCO₂e per year (100k total 2026-2030)
ADDITIONAL: Feedstock sustainability certification, production facility details, application/storage documentation

EXCLUDED: Same as ARR — no OFAC, no unapproved methodologies
DEADLINE: Same as ARR

Soil Carbon RFP Overview

EY Soil Carbon Removal Projects — Feb 2026
Same procurement cycle as ARR and Biochar. Focused on soil carbon removal projects.

ACCEPTED METHODOLOGIES: VM0042 Improved Agricultural Land Management, Climate Action Reserve U.S. Soil Enrichment Protocol
STANDARDS: VCS (Verra), Climate Action Reserve (CAR)
CARBON PROFILE: Removals only
VOLUME: 10,000 – 60,000 tCO₂e per year per project
CO-BENEFITS: Supply chain sustainability, biodiversity, improved livelihoods preferred
PAYMENT: Prepayment discount offers encouraged alongside payment on delivery (50 days)
RATINGS: Preferred B+ (Sylvera/BeZero) or Tier 2 (Calyx). Not required.
GEOGRAPHIES: EY operational countries preferred (150+). OFAC excluded.

DEADLINE: Same as ARR/Biochar — 27 February 2026
SHORTLIST: Week of 2 March | DD: Week of 6 April | VERPA: June 2026
