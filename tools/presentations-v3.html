📎 Next step — add these slides to your existing deck

Follow these 5 steps in PowerPoint. It takes about 30 seconds.
1
Open your existing ClearSky deck in PowerPoint on your computer.
2
Click Insert in the top menu bar (between Home and Draw).
3
Scroll to the bottom of the Insert menu and click Reuse Slides.
On Mac: Insert → Slide from → Reuse Slides
4
A panel will open on the right. Click Browse and select the PowerPoint file you just downloaded.
5
You'll see thumbnails of the new slides. Right-click any thumbnail and select Insert All Slides.
The new project slides will appear at the end of your existing deck. Done!
💡
PowerPoint handles the formatting automatically when you use Reuse Slides — your existing deck's design stays intact.
